Missouri Headwaters Riparian and Rangeland Tool
Welcome! This mapping platform and decision support tool is designed to support rangeland and riparian health management in the Little Missouri River and adjacent headwaters within SD, ND, WY, and MT. Riparian areas are among the most critical habitats within the Northern Great Plains, providing essential habitat for a wide range of grassland birds and wildlife. Riparian areas are also essential for the success of ranchers that depend on them for water and forage for livestock, especially during dry periods. The Nature Conservancy in SD, working with partners and experts, compiled spatial data and information regarding landscape characteristics and condition, and developed that information into this online web mapping platform intended to assist conservation partners in the landscape with targeting conservation measures to provide the most benefits at scale.
